Everything JGJPayInventory does for you

A full tour of the app: from the first product added to the invoice sent, by way of the checkout, the alerts and the reports. Nothing is left out.

Module 1

Complete inventory management

A living, precise, traceable inventory, from the catalogue to the physical count.

A customer asks whether you still have a certain item. No more dashing to the stockroom to check: the exact quantity is on screen, and it updates by itself on every sale.

Products

  • Create, edit, delete
  • Image, price, quantity, description
  • Unique item number and unique name
  • Advanced search, filters and pagination
  • Purchase cost and calculated margin (restricted to managers)
  • Internal notes per product
  • Filter by price range

Categories

  • Hierarchical organisation of products
  • Image and description per category
  • A product can belong to several categories
  • Automatic product count

Inventory movements

  • Inbound and outbound with a mandatory reason
  • Traceability: user, date, reason
  • Optional batch number
  • Paginated, exportable history

Physical stocktake

  • Multi-product adjustment
  • Enter the actual count, the difference is calculated automatically
  • Movements generated to stay consistent
  • Reason and batch number

Expiry

  • Expiry date per product
  • Screen for products expiring soon
  • Filters of 7, 15, 30, 60, 90 days and more
  • Colour code by urgency

QR code and value

  • Product QR code for shelf identification
  • Total stock value calculated instantly
  • CSV export of the stock status
  • CSV export of movements with filters
Module 2

Point of sale (checkout)

A modern, fast, frictionless checkout, built for peak hours.

Rush hour, the queue is growing. A few taps are enough to fill the cart, take payment and give change without error, even on a very busy day.

  • Three resizable areas : categories, products and cart, adjustable to your screen.
  • Add to cart with highlighting and automatic scrolling to the item.
  • Payment with up to two methods combined, with change calculation.
  • Real-time stock check before confirming the payment.
  • PDF receipt offered and printed after each successful sale.
  • Sales history filterable, with PDF and CSV export.
  • Automatic stock update on every payment, and restored on cancellation.
Module 3

Professional invoicing

Compliant documents, computed server-side, ready to be printed or sent.

A business customer asks for a proper invoice. No more typing it by hand at the risk of a VAT error: it comes out in one click, correct and in your name.

  • Automatic generation of the invoice on payment.
  • VAT and discounts per item and per sale.
  • Net / VAT / gross summary clear and exact.
  • Automatic numbering with a custom prefix.
  • List and detail of invoices with filtering by status.
  • Printing, sending and cancellation of invoices.
  • Complete header : logo, contact details, invoice footer, terms and bank details.
Module 4

Quotes and sales proposals

Price an offer, hand your customer a clean document, then turn it into a sale in a single click - without re-entering anything.

A customer hesitates and wants a price in writing. You hand them a clean quote in two minutes; if they say yes, it becomes a completed sale in one click, with nothing to retype.

  • Create a quote from the checkout, without taking payment or touching the stock.
  • Professional PDF to print, send or share - ready to hand to the customer.
  • One-click conversion to a sale, with immediate payment.
  • Quote number dedicated, with a validity date.
  • Clear statuses (draft, sent, accepted, converted); a converted quote is locked.
  • Net / VAT / gross calculation and discounts, identical to the invoice.
Module 5

Sending documents by email

Send the invoice or quote directly to the customer by email, in one click. No more paper, no more waiting: everything is digital.

The customer has already left and asks again for their invoice. Instead of reprinting it and chasing after them, you email it to them, right away, from the app.

  • Invoice and quote sent by email to the customer, with the professional PDF in their name.
  • Directly from the checkout, a sale's detail or the document screen - without switching windows.
  • Reliable, non-blocking delivery : automatic retry on a network drop, the email is never lost.
  • Dedicated email configuration per environment, ready for a professional provider (high deliverability).
  • Inventory alerts and password resets also by email, in your company's branding.
Module 6

Customer loyalty programme

Turn a one-off purchase into a lasting relationship: your customers earn points and use them as a discount. Just like the biggest brands.

Your loyal customer comes back every week. Her points add up by themselves and give her a real reason to return to you rather than to the shop across the street.

  • Points on every purchase, added automatically to the customer's record.
  • Points redeemable as a discount right at the moment of sale.
  • Balance and history visible on the customer record (earnings and redemptions).
  • Fully configurable per company: earning rate, value of a point, minimum to redeem.
  • Multi-currency : the point value is expressed in your currency (EUR, FCFA, USD...).
  • Optional : enable it if you wish. When disabled, it is completely invisible.
Module 7

Real-time alerts and notifications

Never be caught out by a stockout again. The app warns you at the right time.

Your best seller runs out on a Saturday, and every stockout is a lost sale. The app warns you before the shelf is empty, so you can order in time.

Configurable thresholds

  • Warning threshold (orange) and critical threshold (red)
  • Global thresholds per company or specific per product
  • Smart defaults if nothing is set

Real time

  • Instant in-app notifications for admins and managers
  • Inventoryout banner and pulsing alert cards
  • Notification filters by type (stock, system)

Emails and preferences

  • Email alert on critical stock
  • Three alert types: critical, low, daily summary
  • Each user chooses whether or not to receive emails
Module 8

Dashboards and reports

Turn your data into decisions. Clear charts and precise indicators, from the day to the year.

At month-end, the real question: did it work? At a glance, you see your best sales and how your revenue is trending, without lining up a single figure by hand.

  • Today's summary : revenue, number of sales and products out of stock.
  • Sales trend curve : a trend chart to visualise activity over time.
  • Top products sold (5, 10, 20 or 50) by day, week, month, quarter, half-year, year or custom period.
  • Évolution du chiffre d'affaires compared with the previous month, expressed as a percentage.
  • Breakdown chart (pie) of stock by category.
  • Inventory movement chart : inbound and outbound over today, 7 days and 30 days.
  • Transactions dashboard with tabs: overview, list and reports.
  • Inventory report : stock value by category, stockouts and alerts.
  • Clickable KPI cards that lead straight to the relevant screen.
  • PDF and CSV export of sales, movements and inventory.
Module 9

Purchasing and suppliers

Record your purchases and your goods receipts. The cost actually paid feeds your stock and your accounting, with no double entry and no guesswork.

The supplier delivers and hands you their invoice. You record it once: stock rises, the real cost is taken into account and your deductible VAT is already counted.

  • Supplier file complete: create, edit, activate and deactivate, with server-side search and filters.
  • Purchase documents (goods receipt or supplier invoice) : choice of supplier and product lines with quantity, real cost and VAT.
  • Real cost captured at source : each purchase updates the product's weighted average cost. No more approximate purchase prices.
  • Deductible VAT recorded automatically, so you know the VAT actually due (collected minus deductible).
  • Inventory entries generated on receipt: stock and its value stay accurate at all times.
  • Supplier reference, date, currency and notes ; totals computed server-side, never on the client device.
Module 10

Accounting and financial steering

A genuine financial dashboard, computed server-side to the cent. Profit, VAT and cash flow over the period of your choice, ready to export.

Your accountant asks for your figures. Instead of spending the weekend on it, you produce the period's profit, VAT and cash flow in one click, accurate to the cent.

Financial dashboard

  • Net revenue, gross margin and cost of sales
  • VAT due, net cash flow and stock value at cost
  • Indicators shown immediately, data loaded in the background

Income statement

  • Net revenue, cost of sales, gross margin and margin rate
  • Gross receipts: gross revenue, credit notes, net revenue, collected VAT
  • Clear distinction between net profit and gross receipts

VAT

  • VAT collected, deductible and due, correctly signed
  • VAT credit detected when deductible exceeds collected
  • Breakdown by rate: net base, collected, deductible, due

Cash flow

  • Inflows, outflows and net balance over the period
  • Breakdown by payment method (cash, card, transfer, mobile)

Filters and periods

  • Periods of day, week, month, quarter, year or custom
  • Comparison with the previous period, variation as a percentage
  • Combinable filters (cashier, product, customer, payment, rate, amount) and sales drill-down, always server-side

Exports and official documents

  • Formal PDF and CSV export of each report
  • Full (annual) report in a single document: profit, VAT, cash flow
  • Legal identity (company name, registration, VAT number, capital) and a unique document number

Weighted average cost method (compliant with international standards), amounts calculated exactly, configurable fiscal year and multi-rate VAT. Module restricted to administrators and managers.

Module 12

Tailored roles and permissions

Three ready-to-use roles (administrator, manager, cashier) and, above all, tailored per-user rights : grant or revoke a specific permission for a person, beyond their role. Everything is verified server-side.

You trust one manager to edit products, but not the others; you allow a specific cashier to issue refunds. In two clicks, each person has exactly the rights you decide - no more, no less.

Beyond roles: fine-grained rights, person by person

Roles are only a starting point. The administrator then adjusts each user individually.

Grant or revoke a right

  • Give a person a right their role doesn't have (e.g. a manager who can edit or delete products).
  • Or remove a right the role has by default (e.g. a cashier who can no longer issue refunds).
  • The change affects only that person, never the others with the same role.

A clear matrix by module

  • Rights grouped by module: Products, Sales, Inventory, Customers, Invoices, Purchasing, Reports, Accounting, Users...
  • One checkbox per action (view, create, edit, delete, refund...). Tick it, it's applied.
  • Each right shows whether it is already included by default in the role.

Safe, traced, no lock-out

  • Always verified server-side : impossible to bypass from the app.
  • Anti-lock-out : an administrator always keeps control (you can't remove management from the last admin).
  • Every change to rights is recorded in the audit log.

A dedicated Superadmin console also lets you manage all the companies on the platform.

Security, reliability and technology

A solid technical foundation to protect your data and support your growth.

Your data is years of work. It stays strictly yours, isolated from other companies, protected and traced, whatever happens.

Watertight multi-company

Data strictly partitioned per company. No leakage possible between customers.

Token authentication

Secure token-based sign-in and permissions verified on every sensitive action.

Audit log

Sign-ins, creations, edits and stock movements are traced and timestamped.

Protected passwords

Hashed passwords, one-time reset codes and rate-limited attempts.

Multi-platform

Mobile, desktop and web from a single codebase, for an identical experience everywhere.

Microservices architecture

Biometric sign-in

Reliable real-time notifications
