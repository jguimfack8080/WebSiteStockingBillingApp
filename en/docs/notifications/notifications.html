Notifications and alerts

All roles

Receive alerts (low stock, out of stock, system), read them and set their preferences.

Presentation

Notifications keep you informed of important events: low stock, out of stock, and system messages. They prevent bad surprises (unseen stock-out).

They arrive in real time: as soon as an event happens, the alert appears (and the sound plays if enabled), whatever screen you are on. If the connection is briefly lost, missed alerts are caught up automatically once it is restored: no notification is lost.

Access

The notification center opens via the bell icon at the top of the dashboard (manager / admin). Preferences are available to everyone via Settings > Notification preferences.

Interface

  • Filters (unread / all, by type): Show unread or the whole history; filter by type (stock, system).
  • Mark as read / Mark all as read: Manage what has been seen.
  • Preferences (alert types + e-mail): Enable e-mail reception per type (critical stock, low stock, daily digest); in-app is always active.
  • Notification sound: A sound plays when a new notification arrives, whatever screen you are on in the app. You can turn it on or off in Settings > General > "Notification sound"; a "Test" button (play icon) next to it lets you hear the sound at any time. This setting is specific to this device.
  • Daily digest (days, time, timezone): Schedule a daily summary by e-mail.

Step-by-step procedure

  1. Open the notifications bell (manager / admin). -> The notifications list appears, unread ones highlighted.
  2. Tap a notification to mark it read, or "Mark all as read". -> The unread counter decreases.
  3. In Settings > Notification preferences, enable e-mail for the wanted types and set the daily digest. -> You will receive the chosen alerts by e-mail, in addition to in-app. (Check: Check that at least one day is selected for the digest.)

Examples

Low stock alert: A product drops below its threshold: a notification appears; you tap the bell and see the product.

Daily digest at 8 a.m.: You enable the digest, choose weekdays and 08:00: every morning you get a summary by e-mail.

In-app notifications are always active; only e-mail sending is configurable.

Error messages

Select at least one day
Cause: The daily digest is enabled but no day is checked. - Solution: Check at least one day, or disable the digest.

Tips

  • Enable e-mail at least for critical stock: it is the alert you must never miss.
