Analyze transactions

AdminManager

Track received payments, filter, sort and see averages.

Presentation

The transactions dashboard shows received payments, with indicators (average amount per transaction) and filters by period, amount and payment method.

IMPORTANT - a SALE and a TRANSACTION are not the same. A sale can be settled by SEVERAL payments (e.g. part in cash, part by card): each payment counts as ONE transaction. The number of transactions is therefore always GREATER THAN OR EQUAL to the number of sales. Concrete example: 150 sales of which 60 paid in 2 parts = 210 transactions. This is not an error: the "Today's overview" screen counts SALES (150), while this dashboard counts payment TRANSACTIONS (210).

Access

From the dashboard, Finance section, "Transactions" card.

Interface description

  • Average amount per transaction: Summary indicator of activity.
  • Distribution by payment method (donut): In the Overview tab, a donut chart shows the share of each payment method (cash, card, transfer...). The percentage is shown directly inside each slice and the period total in the center. Tap a slice to see, in the center, the related method and its percentage. A legend beside it details each method with its amount and percentage.
  • Filters and sorting: By period, amount range, payment method; sort by amount.

Step-by-step procedure

  1. Action: Open "Transactions" and choose a period. Result: The indicators and list update.
  2. Action: Refine with filters (amount, payment method) and sorting. Result: You isolate the transactions to analyze.

Tips

  • Compare the average per transaction across periods to track the trend.
  • Very small donut slices do not show their percentage inside (to stay readable): find their exact value in the legend next to the chart.
