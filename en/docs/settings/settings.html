Adjust settings (language, theme, notifications)

All roles

Change language, light/dark mode, manage notifications and access settings.

Overview

Settings gather general options (language, light/dark mode), notification preferences, profile access, and, depending on the role, company settings and stock thresholds.

Access

Tap the "Settings" icon or card.

Interface description

  • General: language and dark mode: Choose Francais / English / Deutsch and toggle light/dark.
  • Notifications: Alert preferences (stock, etc.).
  • Account / Profile: Access to your information and password.
  • Company and stock thresholds (per rights): Company settings and alert thresholds, available depending on the role.

Step-by-step procedure

  1. Open settings. -> The different sections appear.
  2. Change the language or enable dark mode in the General section. -> The change applies immediately to the whole app. (Check: Check that the display matches your choice.)

Special cases

Company settings (logo, legal identity) are generally reserved for the administrator.

Tips

  • The language and theme choice is personal to your session and does not affect other users.
